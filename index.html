About MinMax Talent Center

Minmax Talent Centre (MTC) is a legally Creative Entrepreneurship venture situated in Nyeri Town CBD. It is a component under the larger Minmax Group. In general, the Minmax Group is an enterprise that works towards creative entrepreneurship, talent identification/nurturing and holistic development of individuals-and especially the young people. The Group designs and implement programs to facilitate the professional, entrepreneurial and personal growth of young people aged 15-25 years old, among others. Minmax Group also seeks to promote awareness and advocacy for dyslexia as learning condition. Visit our dyslexia advisory page

Minmax is so much more than a music school. We are a group of music enthusiasts who have banded together to share our love of music and musical instruments. Our language is music. It's a form of treatment for us! We think that learning to play the piano,drums, guitar, singing, and other instruments should be enjoyable, and our trainers are eager to guide you on your musical journey.
